Conference 2025

Conference Themes

  • Agro Machinery: Design and Development

    Focus on innovative designs, development, and advancements in agricultural machinery to enhance productivity and sustainability.

  • Soil and Water Conservation Engineering Techniques in Agriculture

    Explore techniques for efficient soil and water conservation, including irrigation methods, soil fertility management, and erosion control.

  • Food, Value Addition, Storage & Post-harvest Technologies

    Discuss advancements in food processing, value addition, storage solutions, and post-harvest technologies to reduce food wastage and improve quality.

  • Renewable Energy & Biotechnology in Agriculture

    Investigate the role of renewable energy and biotechnology in enhancing agricultural productivity and sustainability.

  • Automation, AI, IoT and Robotics in Digital Agriculture

    Examine the integration of automation, artificial intelligence, Internet of Things, and robotics in modern agriculture to increase efficiency and precision.

  • Urban Horticulture and Landscape

    Focus on urban horticulture practices and landscape design to promote green spaces in urban areas and enhance environmental quality.

  • Sustainable Agriculture, Climate Resilient and Precision Farming

    Explore sustainable agricultural practices, climate-resilient farming techniques, and precision farming methods to address the challenges of climate change.

  • Food Sciences and Dairy Technology

    Discuss the latest research and innovations in food sciences and dairy technology to improve food quality, safety, and nutrition.

  • Entrepreneurship in Emerging Markets: Challenges and Opportunities

    Analyze the challenges and opportunities for entrepreneurship in emerging markets, with a focus on agricultural enterprises and startups.
